Tokyo

Tokyo, Japan, a futuristic city, captivates with its skyline, diverse neighborhoods, and vibrant culture. From art to sumo matches and iconic sites like the Imperial Palace, Tokyo offers an ever-changing, unique experience. Planning for popular attractions is essential to fully embrace its charm.

Gyeongbokgung Korea

Explore Gyeongbokgung Palace, Seoul's iconic 600-year-old marvel, showcasing Joseon dynasty grandeur. Despite damage by Imperial Japan, it's a must-see, housing The National Palace Museum and offering a glimpse into Korea's rich history. Visit on weekdays to avoid crowds.

Great Wall of China

Discover China's iconic Great Wall, a UNESCO World Heritage Site and New Seven Wonders of the World. Explore its ancient architecture and breathtaking scenery. Near Beijing, Jinshanling and Mutianyu offer hiking and family-friendly climbs, while Simatai provides a unique night tour.
